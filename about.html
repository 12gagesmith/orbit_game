    Orbit Game, based on Orbito, is a game where you try to get four marbles of your colour in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row on a shifting game board. But watch out! At the end of your turn, ALL marbles shift 1 position on their orbit! Moreover, you can also disrupt your opponent's strategy by moving one of their marbles on your turn.
